Our Story

Born from crisis,
built for change.

Terra Nova was founded in 2008 after witnessing the devastation of the Amazon drought and its ripple effects on indigenous communities. What began as a small team of 12 scientists and activists has grown into one of the world's most trusted environmental NGOs.

We operate at the intersection of ecological restoration and human dignity — because you cannot save the earth without first caring for its people.

🌍
Mission

Restore ecosystems and empower communities to thrive in balance with nature.

🔭
Vision

A world where every community has the resources and knowledge to protect its environment.

⚡
Values

Transparency, courage, science-led action, and radical inclusion at every level.
