Our Mission

Terra Nova was founded in 2008 after witnessing the devastating Amazon drought and its cascading effects on indigenous communities. What began as a team of 12 scientists and activists has grown into one of the world's most trusted environmental NGOs — operating in 142 countries and touching the lives of over 2.4 million people.

We operate at the intersection of ecological restoration and human dignity — because you cannot save the earth without first caring for its people. Every programme we run is science-led, community-driven, and held to rigorous transparency standards.
